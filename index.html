Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my property pressure washed?

We recommend annual pressure washing for most properties. However, high-traffic areas or properties with heavy algae growth may benefit from bi-annual cleaning. Paver sealing should be done every 2-3 years for optimal protection.

Is pressure washing safe for all surfaces?

We use appropriate pressure levels and techniques for each surface type. Our experienced team knows how to safely clean delicate surfaces like roofs and painted areas while effectively removing dirt and stains from harder surfaces.
